The First Family We Are Helping

In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate

The first family we hope to help through the One Dollar Exit project is a Muslim family of eight people: a father, a mother, five children, and the wife's mother.

For more than 10 years, this family has been living in a rented apartment.

The father works constantly and tries to do everything possible to provide for his wife and children. Despite all his efforts, their financial situation has remained very difficult for many years.

The greatest dream of this family is very simple — to have their own home and to free themselves from the debts that have been weighing on their lives for a long time.

A family that has tried many times to find help

For many years this family has contacted different charitable organizations hoping to receive help.

But they were often refused.

The reason was that the head of the family owns a car.

Some people assumed that if someone has a car, it means they are not truly in need.

But the reality is very different.

The real situation

The car is not a luxury.

It is a necessity for the family's life.

The father uses the car not only to transport the family but also sometimes to earn additional income in order to improve their situation and support his children.

Selling the car would not solve their problems.

Even if it were sold, the money would not be enough to buy even the smallest apartment. At the same time, without the car the father would lose the ability to earn additional income.

A family worthy of support

This is a hardworking and honest Muslim family.

The father works a lot. The mother takes care of the children and the home.

They live very modestly and try to manage their lives through their own efforts.

They do not have relatives who can significantly help them.

After many years of hardship, they still hope that Allah will open a door of relief for them.

Our goal

We hope to help this family overcome the difficulties they have faced for many years.

With the support of the Muslim community, we hope to:

  • help them obtain a small apartment
  • help them repay their debts
  • give their children a more stable and peaceful future

If Allah wills, it would be a great blessing if this family could receive help before the Eid celebration.

Become a reason for relief for this family

Allah often helps people through other people.

Perhaps your sadaqah will become the reason this family finally finds relief after many years of hardship.

Every contribution matters.

Even a small act of charity brings this family closer to a new life.

May Allah reward everyone who helps those in need.
