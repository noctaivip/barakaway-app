Prophet’s Duas ﷺ

A carefully curated collection of powerful supplications made by Prophet Muhammad ﷺ for forgiveness, protection, guidance, provision, and well-being in this life and the next.

Dua Collection

1
Forgiveness
Source: Ibn Majah 3850
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي
Transliteration
Allahumma innaka ‘afuwwun tuhibbul-‘afwa fa‘fu ‘anni.
Meaning

O Allah, You are Forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me.

2
Forgiveness & Well-being
Source: Ibn Majah 3871
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْعَفْوَ وَالْعَافِيَةَ
Transliteration
Allahumma inni as’alukal-‘afwa wal-‘afiyah
Meaning

O Allah, I ask You for forgiveness and well-being.

3
Useful knowledge
Source: Ibn Majah 925
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَافِعًا
Transliteration
Allahumma inni as’aluka ‘ilman nafi‘an
Meaning

O Allah, grant me beneficial knowledge.

4
Guidance
Source: Muslim
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْهُدَى
Transliteration
Allahumma inni as’alukal-huda
Meaning

O Allah, grant me guidance.

5
Halal provision
Source: Tirmidhi
اللَّهُمَّ اكْفِنِي بِحَلَالِكَ
Transliteration
Allahumm-akfini bihalalika
Meaning

O Allah, suffice me with what is lawful.

6
From anxiety
Source: Bukhari
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ
Transliteration
Allahumma inni a‘udhu bika minal-hamm
Meaning

O Allah, protect me from anxiety.

7
Good in both worlds
Source: Bukhari & Muslim
اللَّهُمَّ آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً
Transliteration
Allahumma atina fid-dunya hasanah
Meaning

O Allah, grant us good in this world and the next.

8
Protection from loss
Source: Muslim
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ زَوَالِ نِعْمَتِكَ
Transliteration
Allahumma inni a‘udhu bika min zawali ni‘matika
Meaning

O Allah, protect me from loss of blessings.

9
Protection from punishment
Source: Muslim
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ عَذَابِ جَهَنَّمَ
Transliteration
Allahumma inni a‘udhu bika min ‘adhabi jahannam
Meaning

O Allah, protect me from Hellfire.
