About One Dollar Exit

In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate

Why this project exists

One Dollar Exit is a charity initiative created to help Muslim families who are facing serious financial and life difficulties.

Our mission is simple: to help families who are struggling to find a way out of hardship.

These may be families who live in unstable housing, families facing heavy debts, or families raising children in extremely difficult situations.

We believe that when the Muslim community comes together, even the smallest contribution can change someone's life.

Why it is called “One Dollar Exit”

The name represents a simple idea: sometimes even a very small act of charity can become the reason someone escapes a difficult situation.

However, the name does not mean donations are limited to one dollar.

  • Some people may give a little
  • Some people may give more
  • Both are meaningful and valuable

Even $1 of sadaqah can bring relief to a struggling family and bring blessings to the one who gives.

How the project works

The platform follows a very simple model:

  • One family
  • One goal
  • After the goal is completed, the next family support case opens

Families experiencing serious hardship are identified and their situations are reviewed through trusted contacts.

Once verified, the case is added to the platform and the community can support the family through sadaqah.

When the situation of one family is resolved, the platform moves to help the next family.

Transparency

Transparency and trust are extremely important for this project.

All donations go to a public wallet address which can be verified directly on the blockchain.

This allows anyone to see the progress of the support being collected and helps maintain openness and accountability.

Respect and dignity

Many people who face serious hardship prefer not to share their personal stories publicly.

This project respects the dignity of families.

Personal information is not published without consent, and some families may remain anonymous.

The goal is to help people — not to expose their difficulties.

Giving in private

Many people prefer to give charity quietly.

Our platform allows donors to support families anonymously if they wish.

What matters most is the intention and sincerity behind the act of charity.

A project just beginning

One Dollar Exit is a new initiative.

We launched it with the hope that, with the help of Allah and the support of the Muslim community, it can grow into a platform that helps many families.

Every meaningful initiative starts with a few people who believe in doing good.

Our goal

Our goal is not only to collect donations.

Our goal is to build a trusted place where Muslims can help other Muslims in difficult times — sincerely and responsibly.

Sometimes even the smallest act of charity can become the reason someone finds a way out of hardship.

And sometimes that small act can change a life forever.

Helping one family at a time.
