Al-Fatihah

الفاتحة‎

The Opening · Surah 1 · 7 Ayahs · Meccan

Al-Fatihah is the opening surah of the Holy Quran. It is recited in every rak'ah of prayer and holds a central place in a Muslim’s daily worship.
Audio Recitation · Al-Fatihah

Al-Fatihah

1
بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
Bismillāhi r-Raḥmāni r-Raḥīm
In the Name of Allah, the Most Compassionate, the Most Merciful.
2
ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ
Al-ḥamdu lillāhi Rabbi l-ʿālamīn
All praise belongs to Allah, Lord of all worlds.
3
ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
Ar-Raḥmāni r-Raḥīm
The Most Compassionate, the Most Merciful.
4
مَٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ
Māliki Yawmi d-Dīn
Master of the Day of Judgment.
5
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ
Iyyāka naʿbudu wa iyyāka nastaʿīn
You alone we worship, and You alone we ask for help.
6
ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ
Ihdinā ṣ-Ṣirāṭa l-Mustaqīm
Guide us to the straight path,
7
صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ
Ṣirāṭa lladhīna anʿamta ʿalayhim ghayri l-maghḍūbi ʿalayhim wa la ḍ-ḍāllīn
The path of those You have blessed—not those who earned anger, nor those who went astray.
