1. Overview

BarakaWay respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed by the BarakaWay app and website, why it is used, and what rights users have.

2. Information We May Process

BarakaWay may process language preferences, theme settings, prayer time settings, device information, app performance data, and information that the user voluntarily provides or allows the app to use.

3. Location Data

If the user grants location permission, BarakaWay uses location data to calculate prayer times, qibla direction, and related Islamic features. Location access can be disabled in device settings.

4. Notifications

The app may send prayer reminders, Islamic reminders, and other notifications selected by the user. Notifications can be disabled in device settings.

5. Local Storage

Some preferences may be stored locally on the user’s device, such as language, theme, saved content, or user settings.

6. Purchases and Subscriptions

If paid features are available, payments are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. BarakaWay does not store full payment card details.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use Google and Apple platform services, system WebView components, notification services, analytics, hosting, or crash-reporting services if they are enabled in the current app version.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Children

BarakaWay is not intended to unlawfully collect children’s personal data. If a parent or guardian believes a child provided personal data without consent, they may contact us.

10. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data where such rights are available under applicable law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The updated version becomes effective after publication on the BarakaWay website.

12. Contact

For privacy questions: admin@barakaway.app
