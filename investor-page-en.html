BARAKAWAY Smart Dhikr Bracelet

The next product in the ecosystem is a smart Islamic bracelet. It is worn as a modern accessory and transforms with a single movement into a tool for mindful dhikr.

  • Larger screen than dhikr rings
  • Higher-capacity battery
  • Men’s and women’s versions
  • App integration
  • Spiritual goals synchronization

Why It Matters

Traditional electronic rings often turn dhikr into mechanical button pressing. BARAKAWAY focuses on mindfulness, convenience, and a modern Islamic lifestyle.

This creates a new category of Islamic wearables and expands the company from an app into a complete App + AI + Wearable ecosystem.
