Stoyanie u ‘Arafata
▾
Ma’nosi

Peredayut, chto Payg‘ambar (sallya Llaxu ‘aleyxi va sallyam) skazal: «Luchshey molboy yavlyaetsya ta, s kotoroy obrashchayutsya k Allohu v den stoyaniya u ‘Arafata, a luchshim iz togo, chto govoril ya, kak i drugie proroki, jivshie do menya, yavlyayutsya slova: “Net boga, krome odnogo lish Alloha, u Kotorogo net sotovarishcha; Emu prinadlejit vlast, Emu — xvala, i On vsyo mojet”».

Transkripsiya

Lya ilyaxa illya Llaxu vaxda-xu lya sharika lya-xu; lya-xu l-mulku va lya-xu l-xamdu va xua ‘alya kulli shay’in kadir.

120
U al-Mash‘ar al-Xaram
▾
Ma’nosi

Peredayut, chto Payg‘ambar (sallya Llaxu ‘aleyxi va sallyam) sel verxom na Kasvu i priexal k al-Mash‘ar al-Xaram, gde povernulsya litsom k kible, posle chego obrashchalsya k Allohu s molbami i proiznosil slova «Alloh velik», «Net boga, krome Alloha» i «Net boga, krome odnogo lish Alloha, u Kotorogo net sotovarishcha», poka ne rassvelo polnostyu, a potom dvinulsya v put eshchyo do vosxoda solntsa.

MuzdalifaMash‘arXadj
121
Brosanie v stolbi
▾
Ma’nosi

Peredayut, chto, brosaya kameshek v kajdiy iz tryox stolbov, Payg‘ambar (sallya Llaxu ‘aleyxi va sallyam) proiznosil slova «Allohu akbar», posle chego proxodil vperyod, ostanavlivalsya, vozdevaya ruki k nebu, i vzival k Allohu, obrativshis litsom k kible. On postupil tak, brosiv kameshki v perviy i vtoroy stolbi. Chto je kasaetsya bolshogo stolba, to, brosaya v nego kameshki, on takje vsyakiy raz proiznosil slova «Allohu akbar», no potom pokinul eto mesto i ne stal stoyat u etogo stolba.
